Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2025

HasMedia Intelligence ("HasMedia", "we", "us", or "our") operates the hasmedia.ai platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your social media accounts, we collect:

  • Your name and email address from Facebook/Instagram
  • Instagram and Facebook page analytics data (reach, engagement, impressions)
  • Post performance metrics
  • Audience demographic data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Display your social media analytics in the HasMedia dashboard
  • Generate AI-powered content insights and recommendations
  • Identify high-performing content patterns
  • Provide content strategy suggestions

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or social media data with any third parties.

3. Data Storage

Analytics data is processed in real-time from Meta's API. We store only the minimum data necessary to operate the dashboard. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

4. Meta Platform Data

We access your Instagram and Facebook data through Meta's official Graph API. We only request permissions necessary for providing analytics. We comply with Meta's Platform Terms and Developer Policies.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Access to your data is restricted to authorized HasMedia team members only.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your social media accounts at any time
  • Revoke HasMedia's access via your Facebook/Instagram settings

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — required for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or via the dashboard.
