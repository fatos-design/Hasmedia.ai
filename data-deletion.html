Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 15, 2025

HasMedia Intelligence ("HasMedia", "we", "us", or "our") respects your right to control your data. This page explains how to request deletion of any data we hold about you or your connected accounts.

What data we may hold

When you connect a Facebook or Instagram Business/Creator account, we may store:

  • Your account identifiers (e.g. Instagram username, account ID)
  • Access tokens needed to retrieve your analytics
  • Aggregated analytics data (reach, engagement, impressions, post performance)

How to request deletion

To delete your data, email us at:

privacy@hasmedia.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

Please include the email address and/or the Instagram handle associated with your account so we can locate your data.

What happens next

  1. We confirm your request within 2 business days.
  2. We permanently delete the associated access tokens and stored analytics.
  3. We confirm completion within 30 days, as required by applicable data-protection laws.

Revoking access directly

You can also revoke HasMedia's access at any time from your Meta account:

  • Facebook: Settings & Privacy → Settings → Business Integrations → remove HasMedia.
  • Instagram: Settings → Apps and Websites → remove HasMedia.

Revoking access immediately stops any further data retrieval. To also delete data already stored, send the email request above.

Contact

Questions about data deletion? Reach us at privacy@hasmedia.com.
